Life Gym

A private fitness institution before private fitness became a trend.

Life Gym helped shape the private personal training culture of Los Angeles long before boutique fitness became part of the landscape. For more than three decades, clients have come here for a quieter, more experienced, more personal way to train.

The gym is built for people who take their health seriously: professionals, creatives, physicians, executives, active adults, and longtime Westside residents who want discretion, structure, and substance.
